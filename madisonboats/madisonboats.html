Easy Rental

Choose your location, date, time, duration, number of boats. On the day of your rental,
find a space in the park away from others and wait for a text or call letting you know
your boat is ready. Follow the directional signs to get your paddle & PFD.

Three Locations

Three great locations to serve you. Wingra Boats on Knickerbocker street,
Brittingham Boats at Brittingham Place, and Marshall Boats on Allen Boulevard in Madison.

Boats and More

Join us on the lake! We have something for all boat lovers from canoes, to single and
tandem kayaks. Stand up paddle boards, paddle boats, duck paddle boats, as well as
pontoon boat rentals!

COVID-19 Safety

To keep you safe we are:
  • Sanitizing all boats & paddles after every use.
  • PFD's are used only 1 time every 72 hours.
  • All staff will wear face masks or face shields.
  • All bookings are done online to reduce interactions.
